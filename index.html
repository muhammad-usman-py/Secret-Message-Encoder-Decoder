🔐 Secret Message Encoder & Decoder

Welcome to the Secret Message Encoder & Decoder! This tool allows you to encode messages into a secret format and decode them back.

🚀 How to Use

  • Encoding: Enter your message and click "Encode". You will get an encoded message that you can send to someone.
  • Decoding: If you received an encoded message, paste it and click "Decode" to reveal the original text.

💡 Features

  • Encodes messages with a unique pattern.
  • Generates random characters for security.
  • Easy one-click encoding and decoding.
